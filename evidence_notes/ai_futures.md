## What This Source Contributes

AI Futures-style analysis contributes explicit scenario thinking around takeoff speed, governance, lab behavior, and public deployment. ASI Arrival Forecast uses this category for timeline and deployment-delay assumptions.

## Variables Informed

- `takeoff_lag_months`
- `governance_delay_months`
- `deployment_delay_internal_to_public_months`

## How It Enters The Model

The source family informs broad triangular distributions over the lag from AI R&D automation to internal ASI and from internal ASI to public visibility.

## Limitations

- Scenario work is sensitive to author assumptions.
- It may underweight slow institutional, regulatory, or engineering bottlenecks.
- It may overweight vivid narratives relative to base rates.
