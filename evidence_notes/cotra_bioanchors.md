## What This Source Contributes

The Bio Anchors framework models AI timelines through compute requirements and biological reference classes. ASI Arrival Forecast uses this source family as inspiration for explicit, editable distributions rather than point estimates.

## Variables Informed

- `coding_automation_threshold_human_hours`
- General distributional reasoning for uncertain thresholds

## How It Enters The Model

The coding automation threshold is treated as a broad triangular distribution over the size of human coding tasks that agents must reliably complete before AI R&D automation becomes plausible.

## Limitations

- Bio Anchors was not designed as a direct month-level ASI model.
- Compute-needed distributions do not directly imply agency, tool use, or recursive R&D automation.
- The threshold mapping is qualitative in this prototype.
