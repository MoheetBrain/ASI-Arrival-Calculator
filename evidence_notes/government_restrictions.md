# Government Restrictions

target_category:
  - governance
primary_model_use:

## What This Source Contributes

Government restrictions, export controls, frontier model reporting rules, and national-security concerns can affect deployment timing and public visibility.

## Variables Informed

- `governance_delay_months`
- `deployment_delay_internal_to_public_months`

## How It Enters The Model

Governance delay affects the internal arrival calculation when restrictions or coordination slow capability realization. Deployment delay separately models the gap between internal capability and public visibility.

## Limitations

- Restrictions can slow, redirect, or accelerate development depending on context.
- Private and government deployments may remain hidden.
- Policy regimes can change quickly.
