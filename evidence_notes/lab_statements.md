## What This Source Contributes

Public statements from frontier AI labs can signal expected automation of coding, research, product deployment, safety work, and model-assisted AI development.

## Variables Informed

- `ai_rnd_automation_required`
- `ai_rnd_automation_lag_after_agi_months`
- `superhuman_ai_researcher_lag_months`
- `deployment_delay_internal_to_public_months`

## How It Enters The Model

The model samples the fraction of AI R&D that must be automated before recursive acceleration becomes plausible, then scales integration lag by that requirement. Public deployment delay remains separate from internal capability.

## Limitations

- Public statements can be strategic, incomplete, or vague.
- Labs may have private capabilities that are not publicly disclosed.
- Marketing claims and safety claims should not be treated as direct measurements.
