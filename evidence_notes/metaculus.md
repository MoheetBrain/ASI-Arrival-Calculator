## What This Source Contributes

Metaculus provides crowd forecasts on AI milestones. ASI Arrival Forecast uses this source family as a possible external prior and comparison point.

## Variables Informed

- AGI and weak-AGI external comparison forecasts
- External forecast comparison tables in `evidence_tables/outside_forecasts.csv`

## How It Enters The Model

In the AGI-to-ASI split, Metaculus-style forecasts are treated mainly as AGI or weak-AGI outside forecasts. They do not directly prove ASI timing.

## Limitations

- Forecast questions often use definitions that differ from capability-and-agency ASI.
- Crowd medians can move quickly after news.
- The evidence table uses placeholders until specific questions are exported and archived.
