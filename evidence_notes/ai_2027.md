## What This Source Contributes

AI 2027 provides a scenario-style account of rapid AI progress, superhuman coding, AI R&D automation, and takeoff dynamics. ASI Arrival Forecast treats it as a structured scenario input, not as a fact.

## Variables Informed

- `coding_automation_threshold_human_hours`

## How It Enters The Model

The scenario informs the possibility of short lags between strong AI coding/R&D automation and internal ASI. The base model still samples broad uncertainty rather than forcing a 2027 outcome.

## Limitations

- Scenario narratives are not calibrated probability distributions.
- Rapid takeoff assumptions are controversial.
- The public date in such scenarios may be delayed by secrecy or restricted deployment.
